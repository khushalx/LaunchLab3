              Founder briefing

              Welcome to LaunchLab

              Goal

              Build a startup and survive as long as possible.

              What matters

              • Cash: run out and the startup fails.
              • Users: traction and growth signal.
              • Product quality: retention and trust.
              • Market fit: growth becomes more efficient.

              Core idea

              Every decision affects multiple systems. Growth, cash, quality, and reputation pull against each other.

              Simple tips

              • Do not grow too fast without product quality.
              • High price reduces conversion.
              • Control burn before runway gets scary.
